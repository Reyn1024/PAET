# Privacy and Ethics Notes

Real building experiments are treated as potentially privacy-sensitive.

## Data That May Be Sensitive

- Faces or body images.
- Human trajectories.
- Voice or badge/name information.
- Room numbers tied to individuals.
- Work schedules or behavioral patterns.

## Default Controls

- Keep raw real-world data out of Git.
- Blur or crop identifiable visual content before publication.
- Prefer aggregated metrics over individual trajectories.
- Record only the minimum data needed for the experiment.
- Document who can access raw data.
